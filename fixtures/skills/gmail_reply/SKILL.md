# Gmail Reply

Open Gmail in Chrome, find the most recent unread message from a given sender,
reply with a template, and send it.

## Parameters

- `sender` (string) — Email address to search for.
- `template` (string) — Reply body text.

## Steps

1. Open Google Chrome and navigate to https://mail.google.com.
2. Click the Gmail search bar.
3. Type `from:{sender} is:unread` into the search bar and press Return.
4. Click the most recent message in the search results.
5. Click the Reply button at the bottom of the conversation.
6. Type {template} into the reply compose area.
7. ⚠️ Click the Send button to deliver the reply.
